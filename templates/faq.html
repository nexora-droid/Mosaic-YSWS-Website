Frequently Asked Questions

What is this about?

+

Mosaic is similar to Siege but for frontend/backend development!
Every month, a random theme is announced and the participants - which is probably YOU - build a full-stack website/application about that theme! A judging team will review your project in 1 week, and you will be awarded Tiles - our currency - based on the quality of your project!

Is particpation free?

+

Yes particpation is free for all teenagers registered with Hack Club, aged 13-18. Unless your identity is verified you will not be able to access the webpage!

How long will Mosaic last?

+

Mosaic will last for 1 year! Starting January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2026. We look forward to seeing what you make!

Can we work in teams?

+

This YSWS has NO PLANS of ALLOWING TEAMS, as we want true full-stack developers (or aspiring ones) to take part in this, and truly feel happy about what they have done!
We do not recommend working in teams, and this is why our Skip Policy is in action, as you may want to spend time learning first and then making!

What is the judging criteria?

+

For our judging criteria pleaese head over to The Constituion of Tilemakers. To view this document you will need to be part of the Hack Club Slack.

My project hasn't been reviewed for XYZ days/weeks

+

Please note that we have few reviewers who may not be available at all times to review your project. Your project may not be reviewed yet as there may be a break for our reviewing team. Refer to #mosaic-help or #mosaic-bulletin for information regarding delayed reviews.
